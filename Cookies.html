Nexubyte Cookie Policy

Effective date: 21 August 2020

At Nexubyte, we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to improve your experience, analyse site usage and personalise advertising. This cookie policy explains what cookies are, how we use them and how you can manage them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. These files allow the site to remember your actions and preferences (such as language or login information) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-set them each time you return to the site or navigate from one page to another.

2. What types of cookies do we use?

● Essential cookies: These are necessary for the operation of the website and cannot be disabled on our systems. These cookies are usually set in response to actions taken by you, such as logging in, adding products to your cart or completing forms. Without these cookies, some parts of the website will not function properly.
● Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how users use our website, such as which pages users visit most often and whether they receive error messages from web pages. This information helps us to improve site performance and user experience. These cookies do not collect information that identifies the visitor.
● Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalisation, such as remembering your preferences (for example, the language or region you are in). If you do not allow these cookies, some services may not function properly.
Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how users use our website, such as which pages they visit most often and whether they receive error messages from web pages. This information helps us to improve site performance and user experience. These cookies do not collect information that identifies the visitor.
● Advertising and Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver adverts more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement, as well as to help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. These cookies remember that you have visited our website and this information may be shared with other organisations, such as advertisers.
● Third-party cookies: In some cases, we use third-party cookies, which are cookies set by domains other than our website, for example, when we embed media content such as videos or social media buttons that allow you to share content. These cookies are under the control of those third parties and are subject to their own cookie policies.

3. How do we use cookies?

At Nexubyte, we use cookies to:
● Remember your preferences and improve your user experience.
● Analyse traffic and user behaviour on our website to improve its functionality.
● Facilitate the checkout process, such as remembering the products in your cart.
Show you personalised ads based on your browsing and purchase history.

4. How do I manage cookies?

You have the option to accept or decline cookies when you first visit our site. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your web browser settings. Here's how to do this in the most common browsers:
● Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and security > Website settings > Cookies and site data.
● Mozilla Firefox: Go to Options > Privacy & Security > Privacy & Security > Cookies & Site Data.
● Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy > Cookies & Site Data.
● Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Advanced settings > Cookies.
Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience on our website and you may not be able to access some parts of our website.

5. Changes to our Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable law. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.
