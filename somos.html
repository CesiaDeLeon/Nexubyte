Who are we?

Our goal is to remain the leading company in the creation of innovative technologies, consolidating the position we have achieved in our first five years in the market. We are committed to continue promoting excellence and quality in each of our products and services, with the goal of being the preferred option for our customers. We seek not only to satisfy their needs, but also to anticipate them by offering technological solutions that improve their lives and have a positive impact on society.

Our history

Nexubyte was born in Germany on November 21, 2018, by entrepreneur Richard Dubois who is founder and CEO of the company.
The company starts as a small local business that was dedicated to the repair of technology products such as cell phones and computers, in its beginnings Dubois along with his partner Eleonor Muller were the only ones working within the business.
However, a few months after starting Dubois and Muller noticed a significant growth in the business income, which made them consider whether it would be a good idea to expand to other parts of the country. Thus, 3 months after opening their first store, Nexubyte has more than 3 branches in the city of Berlin. Seeing a good acceptance in the city, Richard starts a business idea which tries to create their own products that allow them to have the latest technology and systems.
At the end of 2019, Richard starts with all the processes to create his own products. On January 1, 2020, while everyone is celebrating New Year's Eve, Dubois and Muller are in their office to finalize the last contracts with their suppliers and on January 3, 2020, they start the production of their own smartphones, which are manufactured in the new Nexubyte building.
After starting their production and market research, Dubois and Muller decide to partner with more suppliers to facilitate their production and maximize their quality. On August 21, 2020, one year after its founding, Nexubyte announces its new products in the market which are: high-end computers and headphones.
The news of their new products allows their sales to be high, and the demand for their products throughout the country and outside the country, lead Richard and Muller to seek alternative sales to reach more places.
This is how Nexubyte begins its international expansion process, with its growth in Europe and Latin America.
In November 2020, Nexubyte enters the United States, receiving immediate acceptance, which allowed its revenues to increase significantly, the first sales led the company to produce twice as many items.
Throughout its expansion, the company continued to add technological products such as speakers, different categories of computers and telephones, always seeking to have updated technology.
Nexubyte is one of the most recognized companies for its quality, innovation, wide variety of products and above all for listening to the wishes of its customers, always looking for the best in advanced technology. The company currently has more than 150 stores around the world.
