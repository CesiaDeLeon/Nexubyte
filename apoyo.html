Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if my device gets too hot?

It is normal for devices to get hot during heavy use. If the device gets too hot, close it and let it cool down in a cool place. Avoid using it while charging and make sure applications are up to date.

How can I extend the battery life of my device?

To extend battery life, reduce screen brightness, turn off features such as Bluetooth and GPS when you don't need them, and use Power Save mode. Also avoid leaving your device charging for extended periods of time.

What should I do if my Samsung device won't turn on?

First, try charging the device for at least 30 minutes. If it does not respond, perform a hard reset by pressing and holding the Power and Volume Down buttons at the same time for 10 seconds. If the problem persists, contact technical support.
